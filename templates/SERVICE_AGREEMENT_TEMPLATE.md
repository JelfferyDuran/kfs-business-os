## 1. Scope of Services

Kingdom Financial Services LLC ("KFS") agrees to provide the following services:

### Included Services

- Client onboarding and intake review
- Business-name preparation and availability check
- Formation-provider coordination (e.g., Bizee, LegalZoom)
- New Jersey LLC filing coordination and tracking
- Registered-agent setup coordination
- Virtual-address coordination (optional)
- EIN coordination and application support
- New Jersey Business Registration (NJ-REG) coordination
- Business Registration Certificate coordination
- Operating-agreement template and customization
- Business-purpose and activity description
- NAICS code selection and review
- One-page business plan template
- Startup budget template
- Banking-readiness checklist
- Compliance calendar (annual report, tax deadlines)
- Final digital document package
- One closeout review call

### Excluded Services

The following are explicitly **not included** and are the client's responsibility or require additional fees:

- Insurance premiums or policy procurement
- Vehicle purchases, leases, titles, registration, inspections, or plates
- Local permits, licenses, zoning variances, or municipal fees
- Attorney, CPA, tax-preparer, or notary services
- Lender or bank application/approval (KFS coordinates only)
- Credit repair or guaranteed funding
- Ongoing bookkeeping, tax returns, or payroll processing
- Registered-agent renewals beyond the initial setup
- Vendor upsells or services not written into this scope

## 4. Client Responsibilities

The client agrees to:

1. **Provide accurate information** on all intake forms and documents
2. **Respond promptly** to KFS requests (target: within 48 hours)
3. **Make timely decisions** on business name, entity structure, and registered agent
4. **Pay fees** according to the schedule above
5. **Maintain accurate records** of entity documents after formation
6. **Comply with ongoing obligations** (annual reports, tax filings, registered-agent renewal)
7. **Notify KFS** of any changes to ownership, business activity, or address
8. **Not rely on KFS** for legal, tax, insurance, or lending advice (seek professional counsel as needed)
