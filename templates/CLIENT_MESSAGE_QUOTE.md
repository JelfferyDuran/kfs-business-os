## Objection Handling

### "Why $1,600 when I can do it myself for $200?"

**Response:**
> You absolutely can! Many people DIY their LLC formation. Here's what you get with KFS:
> - Expert guidance on entity structure (single-member vs. multi-member LLC can save you taxes)
> - Coordination with the state and IRS (we track receipts and confirmations)
> - Business readiness package (banking checklist, compliance calendar, business plan template)
> - One expert review call
> - Peace of mind that it's done right
>
> Plus, your time is worth money. If you spend 10 hours researching and filing, that's roughly $1,600 in opportunity cost anyway.
>
> Either way works — just let me know!

### "I need to think about it / check with my business partner"

**Response:**
> Totally understand! Take your time. Here's what I'd suggest:
> - Review the service agreement and pricing
> - Confirm with your partner that the scope is what you need
> - If you have questions, shoot me an email or call
> - No pressure — we're here when you're ready
>
> One thing: if you want to launch by [DATE], we should start soon so everything clears in time.

### "Can you guarantee I'll get funded?"

**Response:**
> I wish I could guarantee that, but here's the honest truth: funding depends on your credit, business plan, collateral, and the lender's appetite. What I *can* do is make sure your business is set up the right way so when you approach lenders, you look professional and legit.
>
> A clean formation actually makes you *more* fundable because you've got your ducks in a row. But the lender makes the final call.
