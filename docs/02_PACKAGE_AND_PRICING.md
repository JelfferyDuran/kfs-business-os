# Package and Pricing Framework

## Recommended offer

### KFS Full Business Launch Package

**Suggested client price:** `$1,600`

This is a business decision, not a government-set price. Adjust only after updating the cost sheet.

### Included

- Client onboarding and intake review
- Business-name preparation
- Formation-provider coordination
- New Jersey LLC filing coordination
- Registered-agent setup coordination
- Virtual-address setup coordination
- EIN coordination
- NJ-REG and Business Registration Certificate coordination
- Operating-agreement template
- Business-purpose and activity description
- NAICS review
- One-page business plan
- Startup budget template
- Banking-readiness checklist
- Compliance calendar
- Final digital document package
- One closeout review

### Excluded

- Insurance premiums
- Vehicle purchases, leases, titles, registration, inspections, plates, or repairs
- Local permits or zoning fees
- Attorney, CPA, tax-preparer, licensing, lender, bank, payment-processor, or notary fees
- Credit repair or guaranteed funding
- Vendor upsells not written into the signed scope
- Ongoing bookkeeping, tax returns, payroll, or registered-agent renewals unless separately purchased

## Payment structure

Preferred:
- `$800` deposit before intake and work begins
- `$800` before final delivery

Alternative:
- Full payment at signing through an approved payment platform

Buy-now-pay-later availability depends on the payment provider and the client's approval. KFS does not guarantee approval and should not provide informal consumer lending.

## Cost-control sheet

Update these figures before quoting:
- Formation-provider plan
- State filing
- Registered agent
- Virtual address
- Expedited processing
- E-signature
- Payment-processing fee
- KFS labor/service margin
- Contingency

## Pricing rule

Third-party fees must be labeled either:
- Included in the flat price
- Estimated and billed separately
- Optional

Never present a vendor's changing price as permanent.
