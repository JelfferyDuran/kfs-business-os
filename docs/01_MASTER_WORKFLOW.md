# Master Client Workflow

## Stage 0 — Lead

**Status:** `LEAD`

Record:
- Client name
- Business concept
- State
- Contact information
- Referral source
- Target launch date
- Preliminary risks or regulated activities

Do not collect SSN, bank credentials, or identity documents at this stage.

## Stage 1 — Scope and Quote

**Status:** `QUOTED`

Send:
- Package summary
- Price
- Included and excluded services
- Payment terms
- No-funding-guarantee notice
- Estimated third-party costs labeled as estimates

Do not begin filing work before written acceptance and required payment.

## Stage 2 — Acceptance and Payment

**Status:** `PAID_DEPOSIT`

Required:
- Signed service agreement
- Deposit or full payment received
- Invoice/payment receipt stored
- Client case ID created

Recommended case ID:

`KFS-YYYY-###-CLIENT-BUSINESS`

## Stage 3 — Secure Intake

**Status:** `INTAKE_PENDING` or `INTAKE_RECEIVED`

Send the intake form after payment. Collect ordinary business details through the form. Collect highly sensitive information through a secure method, not through normal email, text, GitHub, or an unencrypted Obsidian vault.

## Stage 4 — Verification

**Status:** `VERIFYING`

Verify:
- Legal spelling of owner names
- Entity name choices
- State and business activity
- Ownership percentages total 100%
- Registered-agent selection
- Principal and mailing addresses
- Business-purpose description
- NAICS selection
- Vehicle ownership/lease plan
- Whether local zoning, insurance, MVC, tax, or licensing issues require separate action

No filing proceeds while critical information is inconsistent.

## Stage 5 — Formation

**Status:** `FORMATION_SUBMITTED`

Complete through the selected provider or state portal:
- Certificate of Formation
- Registered agent
- Organizer/member-manager structure
- Formation receipt
- Approved formation document

Record every submission date, confirmation number, fee, and source document.

## Stage 6 — Federal and State Registration

**Status:** `REGISTRATION`

Coordinate:
- EIN
- NJ-REG
- Business Registration Certificate
- Annual-report calendar
- Beneficial ownership reporting review, based on current law and applicability
- Sales/tax registration review when relevant

Do not give tax or legal conclusions outside KFS's authorized scope. Flag issues for a CPA or attorney.

## Stage 7 — Operations and Bank Readiness

**Status:** `BANK_READY`

Prepare:
- Operating agreement
- Business description
- Business-purpose statement
- Ownership summary
- One-page business plan
- Startup budget
- Document index
- Banking checklist
- Insurance checklist
- Vehicle-title/registration checklist
- Payment-processing checklist
- Compliance calendar

Describe the business accurately. Never disguise car rental as another industry to influence underwriting.

## Stage 8 — Delivery

**Status:** `DELIVERED`

Deliver a final organized package:
- Formation documents
- EIN confirmation
- State registration documents
- Operating agreement
- Business plan
- Banking-readiness checklist
- Compliance calendar
- Outstanding-actions list
- Vendor log
- Final invoice/receipt

Obtain client acknowledgement of delivery.

## Stage 9 — Closeout and Maintenance

**Status:** `CLOSED` or `MAINTENANCE`

Hermes must:
- Update the client index
- Record final decisions
- Record open obligations
- Add annual-report reminder
- Archive drafts
- Preserve final approved documents
- Remove or redact sensitive data from ordinary working notes
